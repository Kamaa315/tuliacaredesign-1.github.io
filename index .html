About TuliaCare Consultants

A dynamic consultancy firm dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations through holistic development.

TuliaCare is rooted in the belief that given a solid foundation, every individual has the capacity to navigate life and achieve their fullest potential. We partner with corporates, NGOs, churches, schools, and community-based organizations to deliver transformative training and consultancy services.
